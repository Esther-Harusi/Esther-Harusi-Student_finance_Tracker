About

Student Finance Tracker helps students to track their daily spending, stay within their monthly budget, and understand how and where their money goes.

Features include:

  • Add expences
  • Edit expenses
  • Delete expenses
  • Searching
  • Budget cap with visual progress bar
  • 7-day spending trend chart
  • Currency conversion (USD / KES / RWF)
  • Import and export your data as JSON
